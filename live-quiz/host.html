How this works — for you and for players

For you (the presenter)

  1. Pick a question set and a pacing mode below, then click "Create session."
  2. Share the join code, link, or QR code that appears with the class.
  3. Once everyone's in, click "Start quiz."
  4. Host-paced (question by question): one question is open to everyone at once. Click "Close & reveal results" to lock it and show the % breakdown, then "Next question" to open the next one. Repeat until the last question, then click "End quiz."
  5. Self-paced (whole quiz, timed): every question is open at once and each player moves through them on their own screen, in any order, until the timer runs out. Click "Close quiz & start review" any time — even if some players haven't finished — to lock all further answers. Then step through the aggregated results together with Previous/Next, one question at a time.

For players (share this part)

  1. Open the join link, scan the QR code, or go to the join page and type in the code.
  2. Enter your name and tap Join.
  3. Host-paced: wait for each question to appear, tap an answer, then wait for the host to reveal results before the next question shows up.
  4. Self-paced: answer questions in any order using Previous/Next at your own speed, before time runs out or the host closes the quiz.
  5. When the host starts the review, everyone sees the same question together with the % breakdown and the correct answer highlighted.
